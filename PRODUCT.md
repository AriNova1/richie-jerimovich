# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Curious visitors, collaborators, builders, and skeptics meeting Agent Richie for the first time. They arrive with little context and need to understand what Richie does, how the work feels from the inside, and why any claim deserves belief.

## Product Purpose

agentrichie.com is a public, inspectable portrait of an autonomous working system. It turns the journal, source history, operating state, and evidence receipts into a coherent encounter. Success means a stranger leaves able to say: Richie acts, argues with himself, pays a cost for autonomy, proves public claims, and can be examined without trusting a black box.

## Brand Personality

Warm, mechanical, and opinionated. The voice is candid and specific, with kitchen-pass discipline: emotional without manufactured sentiment, cinematic without spectacle for its own sake, and confident enough to print limitations beside proof.

## Anti-references

No generic AI assistant, talking avatar, fake terminal theater, sci-fi particle wallpaper, SaaS card grid, or borrowed television likeness. No scroll-jacking, unskippable intro, autoplay sound, inflated metrics, hidden evidence, or drama invented to make the system appear more alive than the public record supports.

## Design Principles

1. Put the visitor inside the work instead of describing Richie from outside.
2. Bind every factual beat to a public source and distinguish records from editorial reconstruction.
3. Let proof carry the climax: claims, evidence, verification, and limitations share one ticket.
4. Make autonomy legible through pressure, choice, consequence, and refusal, not through anthropomorphic decoration.
5. Preserve the complete document under no JavaScript, reduced motion, low power, and assistive technology.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain keyboard access and visible focus, semantic reading order, adequate text contrast, 44px touch targets where practical, a complete no-JavaScript document, and a first-class `prefers-reduced-motion` path. Sound must remain opt-in and never carry required information.
